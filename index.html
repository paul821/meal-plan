Sunday cook order · fits in ~90 min
Sat PMMix shawarma + gochujang marinades separately. Coat chicken, cover, fridge overnight. 0:00Start lentils boiling in pot (20 min hands-off). 0:05Air fryer batch 1: shawarma chicken breast at 375°F, 20 min. Meanwhile cube all potatoes. 0:25Remove chicken, portion. Air fryer batch 2: gochujang chicken, 20 min. 0:30Assemble salmon lentil bowls cold (lentils done). Wok: flank steak, 4–5 min total, slice and portion. 0:45Remove gochujang chicken, portion. Roll turkey meatballs while air fryer is free. 0:50Air fryer batch 3: turkey meatballs at 375°F, 18 min. 1:05Air fryer batch 4: cubed russet potatoes at 400°F, 20 min (light spray of oil or dry). 1:10Portion meatballs. Portion potatoes across all containers once done. 1:30Done. Only dishes: one pot, one wok, air fryer basket.
